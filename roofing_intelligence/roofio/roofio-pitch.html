Project Context

Job: JHU Science Center

Location: Baltimore, MD

Wind Speed: 115 MPH

Exposure: Category B

System: TPO 60 mil

I have a Carlisle 60-mil TPO assembly. I need to detail the parapet flashing. The wall is 24" high. What is the requirement for the base flashing height and termination?
Based on the Carlisle SynTec Technical Manual (2025) and NRCA Roofing Manual, here is the required detail:

1. Base Flashing Height: Must extend a minimum of 8" above the finished roof surface (NRCA Table 1). Since your wall is 24", the flashing should run up and over the coping.

2. Termination: Because the wall exceeds 18", you cannot simply terminate on the face. The membrane must be fully adhered up the vertical and wrap over the top edge of the parapet wall under the metal coping cap.

3. Wind Zone: For Baltimore at 115 mph Exposure B, your corner zones require FM 1-120 minimum. Verify your edge metal is ES-1 rated.
