## WARRANTY COMPARISON

### Standard Material Warranties

| Manufacturer | Basic | Standard | Enhanced |

### NDL (No Dollar Limit) Warranties

**Requirements (typical):**
- Certified contractor
- Approved assembly
- Pre-job inspection
- Progress inspections
- Final inspection
- Maintenance requirements

**Coverage:**
- Materials
- Labor for repairs
- No deductible (typically)
